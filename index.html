              Our Introductions

              Rights2LIFE

              The University of the Cordilleras aims to promote a responsive criminal justice system through education and research, leading to intermediate changes in human rights-based education, research and development, community outreach, competent faculty and students, multi-stakeholder partnerships, and well-equipped resources.

              • Our Vision

                Philippines as a country with a strong criminal justice system that upholds the rule of law and respects human rights, promoting a sustainable and secure future for all Filipinos. The University of the Cordilleras is expected to play a leading role in achieving this vision through education and research.
